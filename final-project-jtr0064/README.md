# CollectItAll

## Game Concept
**CollectItAll** is a resource collection and progression game. Players explore the world with the ultimate goal of collecting 100% of the scattered resources (such as Apples and Ores). As players hit milestones and complete collection tasks, they earn upgrades to enhance their traversal, collection efficiency, or other abilities.
